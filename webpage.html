About us

Cricket

Cricket is a popular bat-and-ball sport played between two teams of 11 players. The game involves batting, bowling, and fielding, with the goal of scoring more runs than the opposing team. It is played in different formats like Test, One Day International (ODI), and Twenty20 (T20).

Chess

Chess is a strategic board game played between two players on an 8×8 grid. Each player controls 16 pieces, including a king, queen, rooks, knights, bishops, and pawns. The goal is to checkmate the opponent’s king by trapping it so it cannot escape.

Football

Football, also known as soccer, is a popular sport played between two teams of 11 players. The goal is to score by kicking the ball into the opponent's net. It is played worldwide and is known for its fast-paced action, teamwork, and excitement.
